### Limits for Area and Iteration Paths

Limits are an important part of keeping a large global service healthy and happy. In this sprint, we are introducing hard limits of 10,000 per project for both Area and Iteration paths. [Click here](/azure/devops/organizations/settings/work/object-limits?view=azure-devops&preserve-view=true) to learn more about different limits in the service.
